cEDH Copilot — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

cEDH Copilot is built to work entirely on your device. We designed it so there is nothing to hide, because there is nothing to collect.

What we store, and where

Everything you record in cEDH Copilot — games, decks, opponents, opening hands, mulligans, and results — is stored in a local database on your phone only. There is no account, no sign-up, no server, and no cloud copy. If you delete the app, that data is gone with it.

Photos

When you photograph your opening hand, the photo is saved into the app's own private storage on your device. Photos never leave your phone. They are not uploaded, analyzed remotely, or shared with anyone. The app only accesses the camera or photo library when you tap "Snap it," and only with your permission.

Network use

The app makes network requests in exactly two situations, both only when you import a deck:

  • Moxfield (moxfield.com) — to fetch a deck list you paste a link to.
  • Scryfall (scryfall.com) — to fetch card details and one piece of card art per deck.

Both are public, read-only requests. Nothing about you, your games, or your data is sent to either service — only the name or link of the deck you asked to import. Everything fetched is cached on your device so the app works fully offline afterward.

Analytics and tracking

There are none. No analytics libraries, no advertising identifiers, no crash reporters, no tracking of any kind.

Sharing and export

The CSV export feature packages your game history into a file and hands it to the iOS share sheet. Where it goes from there — Files, email, a spreadsheet app — is entirely your choice. The app never sends it anywhere on its own.

Children

cEDH Copilot does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If a future version of the app changes any of the above (for example, optional tournament syncing), this policy will be updated first and the change will be opt-in.
