Keep 7

EVERY KEEP COUNTS

What is this?

Keep 7 tracks what you kept: your opening hand and every mulligan at the table. Then it connects that to who won. Over a few weeks it shows which keeps actually win games. It is not a life counter; it's the notebook you never kept.

Install

  1. Install TestFlight from the App Store.
  2. Tap the invite link I sent you.
  3. Tap Install next to Keep 7. Done.

How to track your games

1

Your decks live on the shelf. Tap + once to import any Moxfield deck by link.

2

Tap New game on Home, then tap the deck you're playing.

3

Tap your seat. Seat 1 goes first.

4

Type a few letters of each opponent's commander and tap the match (or skip any seat).

5

Your opening hand, three ways: Snap it (photo now, fill in later), Tap it (pick the cards now), or Later.

6

Tap it: pick a card type, tap the cards you kept. They collect in the tray up top.

7

Count each seat's mulligans with the steppers, then Start the game.

8

During the game the app stays out of the way: optional round timer, one End game button.

9

Type how it ended in your own words. The bubbles show what Keep 7 understood. Tap ✕ if one's wrong.

10

Skipped something mid-game? It waits in Games to finish, one button per missing piece.

11

Stats: win rate by deck, seat, and mulligans, plus the headline: how your keeps perform.

What we need from you

Log every real game this week. That's the whole ask.

Anything confusing? Take a screenshot, mark it up, and send it right from TestFlight (screenshot → tap the thumbnail → share to TestFlight feedback).

Nothing is too small to report. "This button felt weird" is gold.

F.A.Q.

  • Hand photos are receipts. You fill in the cards later. Auto-reading arrives in the next update.
  • No life counting. On purpose. Keep your dice.
  • Everything stays on your phone: no account, no cloud, no tracking.
